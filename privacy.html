Who We Are

CubanTank Fitness LLC ("Cuban Tank", "we", "us") operates cubantank.com and provides 1-on-1 fitness and lifestyle coaching in Miami, Florida and online.

When you submit our coaching application, we collect your name, email address, phone number, fitness goals, and SMS opt-in consent status.

We use your information only to respond to your coaching inquiry, send coaching-related communications via email, phone, or SMS, onboard you as a client, and send appointment reminders and updates.

By checking the SMS consent box on our application form, you agree to receive text messages from Cuban Tank regarding your coaching application and services. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help. SMS opt-in data and consent are never shared with or sold to third parties.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used solely to provide our coaching services.

We retain your information as long as needed to provide services or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, opt out of SMS by replying STOP, and opt out of emails via the unsubscribe link.
